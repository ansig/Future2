TiY(tm) is developed at the Interaction Design Centre (University of Limerick, Ireland) and is part of a broader research project called FutureCOMM (funded by HEA Ireland under the PRTLI 4 program). 

It is open source and you can find the source code at https://github.com/ansig/Future2. 

Please go to the Settings view by pressing the (i) button in your Profile for more info!
